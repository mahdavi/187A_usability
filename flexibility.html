Flexibility and Efficiency of Use


"Accelerators -- unseen by the novice user -- may often speed up the interaction for the expert user such that the system can cater to both inexperienced and experienced users. Allow users to tailor frequent actions."

Jakob Nielsen

Welcome to our usability evaluation of the website Louisiana.edu. Our main goal for this assignment is to use Neilsen's 10 Heuristics along with our knowledge of brand, function, interactivity, and navigation to successfully diagnose and discuss usability issues with the site.

Our analysis of the site will be contributed equally by each of our team members. For each heuristic, we will be providing a thorough description by answering specific questions which can be grounded through the pictures that we will also be providing. We will not only be focusing on the negatives but the positives as well and utilize both for our evaluation in how to improve the site.

Navigation of our site will be provided on the left with the content of text and picture thumbnails will on the right. The thumbnails will be organized through a slider providing easy access to match the content with the picture. The banner as long with a link on the left links you back to home.
